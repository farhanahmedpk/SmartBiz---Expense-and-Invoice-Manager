## Getting Started

1. **Sign Up / Login:**
   - Go to the start page of the application to `Sign Up` for a new account. You'll need to enter your email, create a password, and provide your business name.
   - If you already have an account, enter your credentials on the `Login` page.

2. **Basic Setup:**
   - Upon logging in, you'll be greeted by your Dashboard. Go to the **Settings** menu via the sidebar to configure your currency, business name, logo, and overall layout theme (Light/Dark mode).

---

## Dashboard Overview

The **Dashboard** gives you a snapshot of your business health.
- **Top Metrics:** Quickly see Total Income, Total Expenses, and Net Profit along with your Active Clients count.
- **Charts:** View beautiful visualizations of your income and expenses over time.
- **Recent Activity:** A quick log of recent invoices and expenses.

*Pro tip: You can rearrange the layout of your dashboard in the **Settings -> Dashboard Preferences** section!*

## Managing Clients

Head to the **Clients** tab to maintain a directory of your customers.
- **Add a Client:** Click "Add Client" to record details such as Name, Email, Phone, and Address.
- **Edit/Delete:** You can manage existing clients using the action buttons on the client row.

### Client Portal
SmartBiz offers an exclusive Client Portal feature, allowing your clients to log in and view their invoices or leave comments.
1. On the Clients page, look for the "Portal Access" setting for a particular client.
2. Provide a temporary password to enable access. 
3. Your client can navigate to the `/login/client` page, log in with their email and the provided password.
4. Once inside, they can view their assigned invoices, their status, and communicate through the comments section directly on the invoice.

## Invoicing System

The **Invoices** tab is the heart of SmartBiz.

1. **Create an Invoice:** 
   - Choose a previously saved client.
   - Assign an Invoice Number and establish Issued and Due dates.
   - Provide Payment Terms (e.g., "Net 30").
   - Define line items, quantities, and unit prices.
   - Apply a tax rate if applicable.
2. **Templates:**
   - During invoice creation, select from curated Invoice Templates (Modern, Minimal, Corporate, Creative, Classic). Watch the Live Preview update on the right side!
3. **Manage Status:**
   - Track if an invoice is `Draft`, `Unpaid`, `Paid`, or `Overdue`.
   - Update its status directly from the list, or select multiple invoices to perform **Bulk Actions** (like updating statuses or deleting).
4. **Comments:**
   - Both you and your client can communicate directly on an invoice via the "Comments" drawer, maintaining a transparent history of adjustments and approvals.

## Recurring Invoices

For retainer clients or regular subscriptions, navigate to **Recurring Invoices**.

- **Create a Profile:** Choose a client and set up line items alongside the "Frequency" (Weekly, Monthly, Yearly).
- **Automated Generation:** The app automatically generates new invoices based on the frequency when the scheduled date arrives. (This runs behind the scenes every day).
- Manage these subscriptions and disable them whenever needed.

## Expense Tracking

Keep detailed records of your spending in the **Expenses** tab.

- **Add an Expense:** Record the Amount, Category (e.g., Software, Marketing, Travel), and Date.
- **Categories:** Add custom categories as your business needs expand.
- Analytics automatically integrate these expenses into your main Dashboard metrics.

## Settings & Customization

Under **Settings**, you have complete control over how SmartBiz handles your workflow:

1. **Profile Configuration:** Update your email, business name, currency symbol, and logo URL so your invoices perfectly reflect your brand.
2. **Theme Configuration:** Switch between Light and Dark mode globally.
3. **Automated Reminders:** 
   - Turn on Invoice Automation. 
   - Define how many days before an invoice's due date an automated reminder should be triggered.
   - The system checks daily and marks reminders as sent to save you time.
4. **Dashboard Config:** Choose what widgets are available on your home screen and in what order.
